Notes on which rates are used:
For NHIS, the household module
For NHANES, the personal interview, conditional on selection into the sample following
completion of the household screening interview
For NIS, the household interview, conditional on having an age-eligible child, as determined from a screening interview, for the landline telephone sample
For NSDUH, the household screening interview
For MEPS, the initial interview, conditional on completion of the prior year NHIS
household interview
For the CPS, the March labor force interview
